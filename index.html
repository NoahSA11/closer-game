How it works

Two rounds. Ten questions. Real answers from your real partner.

1

Set up in 60 seconds

Enter both names, pick Competitive or Cooperative. The game randomly picks who's in the hot seat first.

2

Answer about yourself

One partner answers 5 questions honestly. Their answers lock — completely hidden from the other.

3

Reveal together

Both answers show side by side. Matches earn points. Misses spark conversations. Round 2 flips everything.

Built for real couples

Not your average quiz app

15–20 minutes

Designed to fit your week. After dinner, on the couch, before bed. Short enough to actually do it.

Your real answers

You set the correct answer by answering for yourself. It's personalized to your relationship, not generic quiz logic.

Balanced for both

Neither partner has a built-in advantage. Each round you switch roles — subject and guesser swap every time.

5 categories, 45+ questions

Daily Life, Early Days, Big Dreams, Family, and Just for Fun. New questions every session — randomly drawn.

Two modes

Choose Competitive (someone wins) or Cooperative (shared connection score). Pick fresh every session based on the mood.

One device, no downloads

Pass the phone between turns. No app store, no account, no setup. Open the browser and play.
