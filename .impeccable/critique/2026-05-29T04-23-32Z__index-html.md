## Anti-Patterns Verdict

## Overall Impression

Good bones: typography sophisticated, palette distinctive, handoff/sealed mechanic is the best screen in the game. Problems: no escape from game flow and surface tells signal AI origin.

## What's Working

1. Typography pairing (Cormorant + DM Sans) is confident and premium.
2. Handoff/Sealed flow creates genuine physical ritual — best in category.
3. Color palette (deep forest + rose + gold) is distinctive, not generic SaaS.
